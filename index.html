About Us

Experience You Can Rely On

Brovold & Company LLC is a commercial real estate appraisal and analysis firm based in Mankato, Minnesota. With nearly three decades of experience, we provide credible, well-supported appraisal reports for a wide range of commercial properties throughout Southern Minnesota and beyond.

Our firm is led by Trevor L. Brovold, MAI — Principal and Senior Analyst — whose depth of experience and commitment to professional standards ensures every engagement is handled with precision and integrity.

The MAI designation, awarded by the Appraisal Institute, is recognized globally as a mark of excellence in real estate appraisal and is held by professionals who have demonstrated extensive knowledge of commercial property valuation.

What We Do

Appraisal Services

We provide independent, objective appraisals and consulting services for a broad spectrum of commercial real estate needs.

Commercial Appraisals

Comprehensive valuation of office, retail, industrial, and mixed-use properties for lending, sale, or estate purposes.

Market Analysis

In-depth analysis of market conditions, trends, and comparable data to support sound real estate decisions.

Litigation Support

Expert appraisal services and testimony for eminent domain, estate disputes, divorce proceedings, and other legal matters.

Consulting

Advisory services for investors, developers, lenders, and government entities requiring independent real estate analysis.
