About Us

Experience You Can Rely On

Brovold & Company LLC is a commercial real estate appraisal and analysis firm based in Mankato, Minnesota. With nearly three decades of experience, we provide credible, well-supported appraisal reports for a wide range of commercial and agricultural properties throughout Minnesota, Iowa, South Dakota, North Dakota, and Wisconsin.

Our firm is led by Trevor L. Brovold, MAI — Principal and Senior Analyst — whose depth of experience and commitment to professional standards ensures every engagement is handled with precision and integrity.

The MAI designation, awarded by the Appraisal Institute, is recognized globally as a mark of excellence in real estate appraisal and is held by professionals who have demonstrated extensive knowledge of commercial property valuation.

What We Do

Appraisal Services

We provide independent, objective appraisals and consulting services for a broad spectrum of commercial and agricultural real estate needs.

Commercial Appraisals

Comprehensive valuation of office, retail, industrial, and mixed-use properties for lending, sale, or estate purposes.

Agricultural Land

Expert valuation of farmland and agricultural properties throughout Minnesota, Iowa, South Dakota, North Dakota, and Wisconsin.

Litigation Support

Expert appraisal services and testimony for eminent domain, estate disputes, divorce proceedings, and other legal matters.

Market Analysis & Consulting

Advisory services and in-depth market analysis for investors, developers, lenders, and government entities.
